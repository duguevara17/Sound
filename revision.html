üöÄ Forces Fundamentals

Definition: Push or pull. Measured in Newtons (N).
Gravity: Non-contact attractive force. Pulls objects to Earth center. Gives objects Weight.
Friction: Opposes motion between touching surfaces.
  • Effect: Slows things, creates Heat/Sound.
  • Use: Walking grip, Braking.
  • Nuisance: Wears machinery (Use Oil to reduce).

Weight vs Mass

Mass (kg): Amount of 'stuff'. CONSTANT everywhere.

Weight (N): Force of gravity. CHANGES by location.

Weight = Mass √ó Gravity
